Bring Centres

Civic Amenity Centres or Bring Centres can accept everything from general waste items to electronic goods and even hazardous waste. Most centres will also accept recyclable materials and organic waste. Some items that can’t go in your home recycling bin may be recyclable at a Bring Centre.

13% of all waste found in household kerbside waste binss should be disposed of in bring centres, rather than being put in the black, green or brown bins. Investing in the creation of more bring centres in Ireland will cost the government, but the increase in accesbility will make it more conveinient for everyone to use them.
